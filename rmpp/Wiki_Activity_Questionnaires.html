Wiki Activity Questionnaires

 

Amazon is a company focusing on e-commerce, cloud computing, online advertising, digital streaming, and artificial intelligence. The survey is carried out            by Amazon Singapore but via a 3rd party company, Qualtrics for collection data related to their e-commerce services.

 

 

1. Design and Format

The questionnaire is hosted online, with few questions in one page and it has different question types.

 

Positives

The questions are simple and easy to understand.

 

It has different question types to cater to different data collection requirements

 

Negatives

There is no indication of the number of pages in the questionnaire

 

All questions must be answered

 

Short answer questions like NA are not allowed.

 

Perform product ranking is difficult and time consuming

 

2. Content

The contents are very generic, and the scope/range is very wide.

 

Positives

The questions catered to a large audience

 

Multiple languages are available

 

Negatives

There are many questions to answer

 

Since it is generic, most of the questions may be not applicable.
