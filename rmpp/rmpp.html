Research Methods and Professional Practice

 

Objective

In this module, I will learn what the Purpose of Research is.

The three main reasons/purposes of research are:

1.1: to explore (familiarise yourself with a topic/situation)

1.2: to describe (describe your findings),

1.3: to explain (answer the why)

 

One of the essential approaches in research is the scientific method which involves the following:

-          observation,

-          hypothesis,

-          reasoning and

-          testing

 

This unit focuses on the types of reasoning used in the scientific method, as these are the fundamental approaches for problem-solving.

The two core types of reasoning are Deductive and Inductive reasoning:
